About Me

Hi, I’m Darrik Crawford. I started the UT Full-stack Coding Bootcamp in July of 2021. While completing the program, I simultaneously work at Indeed.com as a Senior Account Executive.

I received my BS in Microbiology with a minor in Biochemistry. These topics were interesting to me in college, but ultimately I decided not to purse these industries. Instead, I joined Indeed in their sales department, and have been working there for nearly four years.

Working at Indeed has exposed me to many different technical roles including SWEs, Developers and more. Solving problems has always excited me, so I started to lean into this coding path. After lots of research, and many conversations with industry professionals, I decided this was my path to a career I would really enjoy.

On a personal level, when I am not studying or completing projects you can likely find me spending quality time with my wife, Krystal, and corgi, Lani. When I need some quiet time, I enjoy nurturing my bonsai tree collection and video games.

I’m excited for this next journey. Please reach out with any questions or comments. I’d love to hear from you!
